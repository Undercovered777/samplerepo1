Step-by-Step Instruction for Online Ordering Automation

Connect your online shop now

Connect Go Live

1. Choose a suitable platform

Choose a platform that suits your business needs and integrates with your existing systems, such as Facebook Live, Shopee Live, or Lazada Live.

2. Set up your online store

Set up your online store by creating a product catalog, and customizing your store's appearance.

3. Automate the order process

Use our webapp to automate the order process, such as automated order confirmation, and inventory management.

4. Integrate with your fulfillment systems

Integrate your online store with your fulfillment systems, such as a warehouse management system, to streamline the order fulfillment process.

5. Test the automation process

After connecting our webapp, test the automation process by placing a test order and verifying that all steps, email confirmations, and shipping label generation, are working correctly.

6. Launch and monitor

Launch your automated online ordering system and monitor its performance to identify any issues and inform us if there's any.
